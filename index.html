Hey there!

We are a passionate music group that blends genres from rock to jazz. Our mission is to cover songs that resonate with everyone in on a party or a pub. To get people up on their feet and dance! We also have a more relaxing setlist for calmer events. We are here to make your event unforgettable. Wheather it's a wedding, private party or special celebration. We are based in Gothenburg, Sweden!

What We Play

From relaxing crowd-pleasing classics to rock-your-shoes-off hits, our setlists are flexible and has it all. Love a specific tune? Let us know!
